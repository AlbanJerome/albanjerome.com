I am an independent strategy and operating advisor working at the intersection of private capital, governance, cross‑border structuring, and AI‑driven decision intelligence.

After a decade in operating and investment leadership roles across venture capital, private equity advisory, AI product architecture, and venture acceleration, I now operate independently. My work focuses on one question: How do you build durable systems that survive complexity, stress, and scale?

I partner with founders, investors, and institutions to structure capital, implement governance, and build AI‑powered decision systems that can operate across borders and under pressure.

<!-- section:focus -->

### Private Capital & Governance

I advise high‑net‑worth individuals, family offices, and multi‑generational enterprises on cross‑border capital structuring, governance frameworks, and long‑term asset positioning. This is architecture work: aligning legal, tax, investment, and operating layers into integrated structures that can carry generational weight. For families navigating liquidity events, relocation, enterprise growth, or succession, I act as a strategic quarterback—bridging advisors, aligning incentives, and protecting continuity across borders and generations.

### Venture & Growth

I work directly with startups and early‑stage companies as an advisor, board participant, and strategic partner. I help founders navigate capital formation, cross‑market expansion, and institutional pressure. The question is rarely just "How do we grow?" It is: What kind of structure can survive growth? How is the cap table built? Who actually controls the company at scale? What governance friction will surface at Series B?
